Target Audience Description:

Our target audience will consist of residents of southeastern Idaho, in the areas around Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven.

Persona - Middle-Aged Mother:

  • Name: Karen Smith
  • Age: 40
  • Occupation: Stay-at-home mom/housewife
  • Married
  • Mother of four children
  • Middle-class
  • Bachelor's degree in Marriage & Family Studies
  • Karen is primarily focused on caring for her family. She spends her time:
    • Bringing her kids to and from school, sports, etc.
    • Making meals and planning ahead for her family for the week.
    • Going to PTA meetings and attending local civic meetings.
  • Karen's environment is her home in suburban Preston, ID. She is familiar with using computers and the internet from her time in college, but she has had limited experience with it since then, outside of activities like banking, shopping, and social media. She has a moderately fast cable internet connection and has a desktop computer, but primarily accesses the internet from her smartphone.

Persona - Elderly Man

  • Name: Samuel Cook
  • Age: 64
  • Occupation: Retired Geologist
  • Widowed
  • Father of five, grandfather of ten.
  • Upper Middle-class
  • Masters in Geology
  • Samuel is primarily focuses on spending time with his family and enjoying his retirement. This includes:
    • Going on picnics with his grandchildren.
    • Fishing out at Bear Lake.
    • Hunting in the mountains nearby.
  • Samuel had to use a computer extensively in his career as a Geologist. He mainly used programs specific to his profession for analyzing rock samples, but is generally comfortable with most tasks on a computer. He generally avoids social media, and his main use of a computer at home is to check his bank account and to plan hunting or fishing expeditions. He has a desktop computer that is his primary method for accessing the internet, along with a smartphone that he rarely uses for anything other than calls and GPS navigation.

Scenarios:

  • Samuel is planning a weekend fishing trip and wants to know if he will need to bring a poncho and other rain gear. The site will give him weekend forecasts that he can use to plan his trip.
  • Karen's youngest son has a soccer game on Saturday, but he is just getting over a cold and she's worried the recent cold weather could make him ill again. The site will let her know if she should have him sit the game out this week.
  • Karen needs new winter tires, but hasn't been able to make the time to do so yet. The site will let her know if the snowy season is starting yet or not so that she can figure out how much time she has to make plans.
  • Samuel wants to take his grandkids on a picnic in a park on the edge of town. The site will let him know if the weather will be clear so that he can make plans.
  • Samuel is planning on ice fishing, and he likes to enjoy it in extreme cold. The site will let him know if he can plan on a nice and chilly weekend for his trip.
