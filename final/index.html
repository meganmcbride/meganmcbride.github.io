What We Do

Temple Inn & Suites was founded in 2007 by members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints with the goal of providing accommodations for members of the church who have traveled great distances to perform temple ordinances. Our goal is to provide an affordable place to stay while visiting the temple, as well as a place that encourages peace and reverence. We know that many of our guests will be traveling with families, so we strive to create a family-friendly environment, and also provide child-care services. This is so that the adult patrons can visit the temple without having to make arrangements for their children. Our goal is to reduce the cost and other burdens faced by patrons who must travel far. We know that visiting the temple can be difficult, but we promise that the Lord will bless you for your efforts to attend. We look forward to your upcoming stay at the Temple Inn & Suites!

Gilbert, Arizona Temple

The building of the Gilbert, Arizona temple was announced on April 26, 2008 by President Thomas S. Monson. Construction began on November 13, 2010 and was officially dedicated by both President Monson and Henry B. Eyring on March 2, 2014. It became the 142nd temple of the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and continues to be a dedicated building where members can find "refuge from the storms of life and the noise of the world."
